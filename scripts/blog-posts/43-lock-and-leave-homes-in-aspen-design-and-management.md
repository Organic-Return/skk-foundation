## Key considerations

**Design for the months you are not there.** A second home needs systems, oversight, and clear procedures when the owner is away. Remote monitoring, vendor access, snow removal, leak detection, and check-in routines can make ownership easier and protect the property between visits.

**Read the governing documents carefully.** HOA rules, rental policies, reserves, pet policies, parking, storage, and planned capital projects can materially change the ownership experience. Before committing, buyers should understand the total cost of ownership and whether the rules support their intended use.

**Budget for mountain ownership.** Snow removal, roof management, heating systems, drainage, landscaping, and security all matter more at altitude.
